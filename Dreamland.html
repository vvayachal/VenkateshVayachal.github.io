Dreamland

An RPG being currently under development

Story

Set in the medival period, this game makes the players walk in a King's shoes who lost everything overnight. Worst part, the King doesn't remember anything about what and how it happened. The players will find out the truth as the King finds out those responsible and takes his revenge.

Features Implemented

  1. Dialogue System
    1. Typing effect to the text as it displays in the dialogue box
    2. Response Box to select responses to each dialogue and trigger events on selection
    3. Easy and Flexible creation and implementation of single dialogues as well as Conversations amongst two characters
