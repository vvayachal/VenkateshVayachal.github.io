Dreamland

An exciting RPG that switches between 2.5D exploration game and 2D platformer

Story

Set in the medival period, this game makes the players walk in a King's shoes who lost everything overnight. Worst part, the King doesn't remember anything about what and how it happened. The players will find out the truth as the King finds out those responsible and takes his revenge.

Features Implemented

  1. Narrative
    • The game is set in the medival period and in a kingdom that was flourishing and thriving
    • The game starts with the king gaining conciousness and waking up to find his kingdom destroyed, loved ones gone, and nothing left of anything or anyone dear to him
    • The king then starts on a journey to look for the responsible party and avenge his kingdom
    • As his journey goes on, he finds his friend and ally who helps him get weapons to aid him on his quest for revenge
    • The player looks for the people responsible and makes his way to the minions' leader (Boss)
    • As the player defeats the boss, they learn a disheartening and shocking news
  2. Movement and Controls
    • Using WASD keys, the player can explore the map and find collectibles
    • Using the left mouse button, the player can attack the enemy while sneaking up on them to make them fall asleep and enter their dreams
    • When in their enemies' dreams, the players will play the 2D platformer game to defeat the enemies and progress further in the story
  3. Visuals
    • Using assets from the Unity store, we enhanced the visual appeal of the game, thus increasing player satisfaction
    • By implementing various animations for the players, NPCs and the enemies, we enhanced the visual experience of the game to the best of our abilities
  4. Combat
    • The player has various attacks when inside the enemies' dreams
    • This improves the gameplay experience of the players while in combat
    • When in the real world, players need to sneak up on the enemies and shoot them with a sleep arrow to make them fall asleep so they can enter their dreams and defeat them. The King is no match for the enemies in the real world so the players must even out the playing field by entering their enemies' dreams where The King has special powers
  5. Dialogue System
    • A Dialogue System that displays the text in the dialogue box with a typing effect to enhance visual effects
    • Response box displays multiple available responses for the player to select which trigger specific events or dialogues on selection
    • Easy and flexible creation of dialogues from the editor itself using a scriptable object in Unity to enable monologues and conversations between two characters
