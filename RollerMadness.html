Roller Madness

A classic roller ball style game

3D Roller Ball game developed using Unity for Windows and MacOS

Features

  1. Movement
    • Implemented the movement of the ball using a script so it rotates as it moves to simulate realistic movement
    • Enhanced player engagement and challenge by addding obstacles to the map. Obstacles are the blocks in the map that restrict the player's movement encouraging them to devise strategies about moving around the map
  2. Scoring
    • Players earn points by collecting coins
    • Every level has a score the player has to beat to advance to the next level
    • The player instantly dies and the level restarts on coming in contact with the enemies
  3. Enemies
    • Designed enemy AI so the enemies follow the players trying to attack
  4. Death Area
    • Player dies when they fall out of the play area and the level restarts
  5. Visuals
    • Enhanced player experience and immersion by adding animation effects to the enemies, player and the collectibles
