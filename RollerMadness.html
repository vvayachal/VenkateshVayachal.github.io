Roller Madness

A classic roller ball style game

3D Roller Ball game made using Unity for Windows and MacOS.

Features

  1. Collision detection and resolution
  2. Multiple Levels
  3. Menu driven UI for this game to reload the level or restart from Level 1
  4. After the player beats the level score, they can move to the next level
  5. Collect coins to score and avoid the yellow boxes
  6. The yellow boxes follow the ball and on collision, the level fails
  7. If the ball falls outside the platform, the level fails
