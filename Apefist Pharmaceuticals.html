Apefist Pharmaceuticals

Fight alongside your simian compatriots against swarms of lethal bacterial and pathogenic monstrosities to save your patient's life

Story

You're an Ape Doctor who has the ability to shrink themselves and enter their patient's body to fight the bacteria and disease causing pthogens to save the patiens life. Dive into your patient and help them survive!

Features Implemented

  1. Stomach Acid Damage to Player's Shield
    • On colliding with the acid in the stomach, player's shield gets damaged by a set amount
    • This can be extended to any on-contact damage objects
  2. Save/Load System
    • Designed and implemented a system that enables the player to save the data in the game and load it whenever needed
    • This system will be extended to save other player stats, collectibles, ability statuses and other level data
  3. Scoring System
    • Designed and implemented a scoring system that helps keep track of the currency (coins) that the player collects
