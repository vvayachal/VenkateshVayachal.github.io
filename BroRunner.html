Bro Runner

2D platformer designed in Unity using open source assets

Features

  1. Movement
    • Using WAD keys to move and Space key to jump, the player (Bro) can navigate the levels and move towards the target
  2. Enemies
    • The enemies will follow the player and will try to attack them when within reach
    • The player takes damage on each hit and loses health. The player dies on losing all health
  3. Scoring and Winning
    • The players collect coins to score and get the trophy to complete the level
    • Competitive players can try to get all the coins to beat the high score. Just want to explore and have fun? Head directly for the trophy to advance to the next level
  4. Environmental Hazards
    • Water and Fire deal damage to the player
    • Players must develop creative strategies to get around these obstacles and win the trophy
  5. Challenging Platforms
    • The game features different types of platforms like rotating platform and moving platform to enhance player interaction and player immersion
  6. Visuals
    • Implemented parallax scrolling to give 3D effect and simulate depth in the scene
    • Using animation timeline and state machines, implemented various player states like win, die, jump, etc. for appealing visuals
  7. Player Prefs
    • Implemented Player Prefs in Unity to enable score tracking and saving game progress
